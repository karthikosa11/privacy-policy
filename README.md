# Privacy Policy — Job Application Assistant

Last updated: April 1, 2026

## What we collect
- Your Google account name and email (for login)
- Resumes you upload
- Job applications you log
- Your Anthropic API key (encrypted before storing)
- Your Google Sheets ID (if you connect it)

## What we do NOT do
- We do not sell your data to anyone
- We do not share your data with third parties
- We do not read your resumes except to send them to Claude AI on your request

## How we store data
All data is stored on secure AWS servers. API keys are encrypted before storage.

Email us at wanderlustbooking11@gmail.com to delete your account and all data.
